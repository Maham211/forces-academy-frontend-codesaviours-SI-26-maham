Admission Information & Process

Follow our step-by-step application process to secure your seat for the upcoming academic session.

How to Apply

  1. Obtain Prospectus
    Pick up an admission package from the main campus processing office.
  2. Complete Application Form
    Fill out your correct biographical metrics and choose your course group track.
  3. Document Verification
    Attach the required academic transcript documents noted in our check-list.
  4. Form Submission & Interview
    Submit your completed form to the admissions desk to schedule your evaluation interview.

Required Documents

  • 4x Recent Passport Size Photographs
  • Copy of Matric Result Card & Character Certificate
  • Copy of Student's B-Form or CNIC
  • Copy of Father’s or Guardian's CNIC
  • NOC Certificate (If from another Board)
Eligibility Tracks

Program Eligibility Criteria

FSc Pre-Medical & Pre-Engineering

Minimum 60% marks in Matric (Science Group) from a recognized regional BISE Board or equivalent credentials.

ICS (Computer Science)

Minimum 50% marks in Matriculation with Science subjects or Computer Studies background.

Matriculation Tracks

Successful graduation pass sheet from Grade 8 or institutional admission test clearance.
